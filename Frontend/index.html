Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I apply for admission?

Applications are submitted online through our portal. You'll need transcripts, test scores, personal statement, and letters of recommendation. The deadline for Fall is January 15.

What is the tuition fee per semester?

Undergraduate tuition is $15,000 per semester. Graduate programs vary from $18,000-$22,000 per semester. Financial aid options are available for eligible students.

When can I register for courses?

Course registration opens two months before each semester. Check your student portal for specific dates and consult with your academic advisor before registration.

Is on-campus housing available?

Yes, we offer various housing options including dormitories and apartments. The housing application deadline is May 1st for priority consideration.
